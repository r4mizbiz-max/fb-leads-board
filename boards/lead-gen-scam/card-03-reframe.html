// The reframe

Nobody wants leads.
They want appointments.

· What you're buying
A lead.
A phone number attached to someone who may or may not remember filling out a form.
→ call · no answer · voicemail · text · silence · retry · dead
· What you actually want
An appointment.
Someone sitting at their kitchen table at 4pm Thursday — waiting for you to show up.
→ calendar invite · confirmed · address · ready to sign
One makes you chase. The other makes you close.
