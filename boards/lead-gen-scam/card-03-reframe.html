The Reframe

Nobody wants leads. They want appointments.

Lead gen companies sold you the wrong product. Here's the difference — and why it matters:

❌ What they're selling you

A Lead.

A phone number attached to someone who may or may not remember filling out the form. Might answer. Probably won't.
→ Call #1 · no answer
→ Text follow-up · ignored
→ Call #2 · voicemail
→ Call #3 · "Wrong number"
→ Call #4 · "Already hired someone"
→ Dead. $75 gone.
✅ What you actually want

An Appointment.

Someone sitting at their kitchen table at 4pm Thursday waiting for you to show up. Pre-qualified. Confirmed. Ready to talk.
✓ Calendar invite · confirmed
✓ Address in the notes
✓ Pre-qualified · homeowner
✓ Budget matched · ready to sign
✓ Exclusive · nobody else pitching
✓ You close. Check cashed.
💡 One makes you chase. The other makes you close. Guess which one lead gen companies want you buying.
