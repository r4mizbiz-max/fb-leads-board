№ 02

The credit
drain.

How it works
Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack charge you per "lead credit" — $50 to $120 a pop. They push junk into your queue so your balance drains whether the lead is real or not.
The dispute trap
Narrow window. Strict reasons. Manual review. Most disputes get denied — "we can verify the lead was delivered." Delivered ≠ real.
The result
You call 40 "leads." 22 never pick up. 8 are wrong numbers. 5 already hired. 5 are price-shoppers. Zero jobs. $3,000 gone.
