The Playbook · Scam #2

The credit-system drain — designed to empty your balance

Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack all run the same model. "Credits" sound flexible. They're not — they're how you lose money without realizing it.

💳

$50–$120 charged per lead — whether it's real or not

You don't pay per booked job. You pay per "match." A wrong number? Charged. Already hired someone? Charged. Ghosted? Charged.

⏱️

The dispute window is on purpose

Narrow timeframe (usually 48-72 hrs). Strict reasons. Manual review. Most disputes are denied with "we can verify the lead was delivered." Delivered ≠ real.

🔁

Auto-reload keeps the machine running

Balance gets low? Card auto-charges. No notification. No approval needed. You just wake up and $500 is gone again.
