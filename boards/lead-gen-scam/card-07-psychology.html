// Why it keeps working on us

Smart contractors keep paying. Here's why.

i.

Sunk cost.

You've already spent $4,200. Stopping now means admitting the money was wasted. So you keep feeding it — hoping the next lead will be "the one."

I've got too much in to quit now.
ii.

Shiny dashboards.

Rising line graphs. Green status badges. "3 new matches!" notifications every hour. Your brain reads it as progress. It's motion, not traction.

It's working — look at all the activity!
iii.

Manufactured urgency.

"3 contractors notified. Claim now." Same psychology as slot machines. Keeps you on the app, keeps you clicking, keeps the credits draining.

Let me grab this one before someone else does.
iv.

Isolation.

Nobody told you a better model exists. Every agency that called you sold the same promise — "we'll get you leads." So you assume this is just the game.

Guess this is just how it works now.
It's not you. The system is designed to feel like it's working.
