The Playbook · Scam #1

The "Exclusive" Lead That Isn't Exclusive

When you buy a "lead" from Angi, HomeAdvisor, or 33 Mile Radius, you're not the only one who got it. Not even close.

1

One form. Sold up to 10 times.

A homeowner fills out one form on one site. That same lead is simultaneously blasted to 4 to 10 contractors in your zip code. "Exclusive" is a marketing word — not a contract.

2

First to call wins. Everyone else pays.

You pay for the lead the moment it's delivered. Doesn't matter if you were call #1 or call #7. Your credit gets charged either way.

3

Why your leads "go cold in 20 minutes"

Because 8 other contractors got the same lead and 3 of them already called. The homeowner is done answering the phone by the time you ring.
