Mistake #3

You hired the wrong person — and didn't know what to look for

Most contractors who "tried Facebook ads" actually tried one of these. Sound familiar?

1

"I boosted a post from my business page"

Boosting is NOT running ads. It's Facebook's way of taking your money with zero targeting, zero strategy. It's like putting a flyer on a random car in a parking lot 3 states away.

2

"My nephew / friend / Fiverr guy set it up"

They probably ran traffic to your website with no lead form, no follow-up system, and Facebook's default settings on — which are designed to spend your money, not get you jobs.

3

"I ran it for 2 weeks and it didn't work so I turned it off"

Facebook needs at least 5-7 days just to figure out who to show your ad to. Turning it off after 2 weeks is like planting a seed and digging it up to check if it's growing.

4

"An agency charged me $1,500/month and I got 3 bad leads"

Most agencies don't specialize in home services. They use the same playbook for dentists, realtors, and restaurants. Your business needs a completely different approach.

✅ None of these mean Facebook ads don't work. It means they weren't set up right. There's a huge difference.
