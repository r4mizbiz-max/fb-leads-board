What Winners Do Different

5 things every successful campaign has in common

These aren't secrets. They're just the things most people skip or get wrong.

1

The ad shows REAL work — not stock photos

Homeowners can smell a stock photo from a mile away. The best ads use real before/after photos, real job sites, and real finished work from YOUR business.

📸 Your phone camera is enough
2

The price is honest and specific

Not "$5,000" or "$10,000" — those feel fake. Numbers like $7,500 or $9,500 feel real because they ARE real. Homeowners trust specificity.

❌ "Remodels starting at $10,000"
✅ "Bathroom remodels starting at $9,500"
3

Leads get contacted within 60 seconds

Not an hour. Not tomorrow. An automated system calls or texts them immediately while they're still on the couch thinking about their project.

⚡ Speed to lead = #1 factor in close rate
4

The form asks if they're a homeowner

This one simple question filters out renters, tire kickers, and people who can't actually hire you. It's the easiest way to improve lead quality overnight.

🎯 One question = 40% better leads
